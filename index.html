A Slack Community for Designers

We are United Designers, a global community of designers and developers who share a passion for creating. We are not for profit.

What you're getting into

Our community has a mix of channels geared towards serious design discussion and professional help as well as channels for day-to-day chatting and inspiration. We always have several ongoing collaborative projects, so join in on those if you want!
